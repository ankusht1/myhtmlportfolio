Best of Di Caprio Movies

My Top 3 movies of all time

  1. Catch Me If you can

    • Plot

      A true story about Frank Abagnale Jr. who, before his 19th birthday, successfully conned millions of dollars worth of checks as a Pan Am pilot, doctor, and legal prosecutor.
      An FBI agent makes it his mission to put him behind bars. But Frank not only eludes capture, he revels in the pursuit.

    • Cast

      • Leonardo Di Caprio
      • Tom Hanks
      • Christopher Walken

    • Director

      • Steven Spielberg

    • Produced By

      • Steven Spielberg
      • Walter F. Parkes

    • Box Office

      • Budget : $52,000,000 (estimated).
      • Gross worldwide : $352,114,312

  2. Shutter Island

    • Plot

      Teddy Daniels and Chuck Aule, two US marshals, are sent to an asylum on a remote island
      in order to investigate the disappearance of a patient, where Teddy uncovers a shocking truth about the place.

    • Cast

      • Leonardo Di Caprio
      • Mark Ruffalo

    • Director

      • Martin Scorsese

    • Produced By

      • Mike Medavoy
      • Arnold W. Messer
      • Bradley J. Fischer
      • Martin Scorsese

    • Box Office

      • Budget : $80,000,000 (estimated).
      • Gross worldwide : $294,944,618

  3. Titanic

    • Plot

      A seventeen-year-old aristocrat falls in love with a kind but poor artist aboard the luxurious, ill-fated R.M.S. Titanic.

    • Cast

      • Leonardo Di Caprio
      • Kate Winslet

    • Director

      • James Cameron

    • Produced By

      • James Cameron
      • Jon Landau

    • Box Office

      • Budget : $200,000,000 (estimated).
      • Gross worldwide : $2,264,750,694
